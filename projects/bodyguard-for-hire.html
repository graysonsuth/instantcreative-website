Bodyguard For Hire

This student run project is a comedy about a guy named Mick who is studying to become a civil engineer, when his friends dare him to become a personal bodyguard, and offer his services on the site HireMe. An artist named Kimmy hires him unsuspectingly knowing that he's a fraud. While guarding Kimmy, an Artist who's work is as good as his name, they face a group of crooks who want to kidnap Kimmy and use him to make art, for them to sell.
